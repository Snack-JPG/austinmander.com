# Website Copy Draft — March 24, 2026

## What changed and why

### Hero
- **Old:** "I build things that actually ship." — Generic. Every dev says this.
- **New:** Lead with the client outcome. You build AI systems that do real work for businesses. The 550K LOC is proof of depth, not the headline.

### Stats
- **Old:** "7+ public repos" / "4 repos in one day" — Nobody hiring cares about repo count.
- **New:** Stats that signal trust: first client closed, production LOC as engineering depth, build speed, and the fact you actually have paying clients.

### About
- **Old:** "Dropped out of law to build software" — Not accurate (gap year), and frames you as a dropout rather than someone who made a calculated pivot.
- **New:** Honest version that positions the law background as an asset, not baggage.

### Services
- **Old:** Generic category labels (AI Agent Development, etc.)
- **New:** Each service describes a concrete outcome a business gets.
